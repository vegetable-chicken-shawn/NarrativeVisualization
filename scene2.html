Potential Fraud and Abuse in H1-B Electronic Registrations

  • H1-B electronic registration began in FY 2021. The selection rate are falling over the years, as the registrations are increasing, while the selected quota remains 85,000 a year.
  • Some applicants are allowed to submit multiple registrations, provided they have multiple employers willing to sponsor them and offer a position.
  • In this undefined area, various companies focus solely on sponsoring applicants without offering a position or running a legitimate business. Applicants pay these companies to increase their chances of winning the lottery.
  • Finally, in FY 2025, USCIS announced that the lottery would follow a "one human, one ticket" rule, regardless of the number of employers submitting applications for the same employee.
